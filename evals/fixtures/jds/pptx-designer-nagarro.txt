Requirements

Proven experience as a PowerPoint Designer or in a similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing creative and visually appealing presentations.
Proficiency in Microsoft PowerPoint, including advanced knowledge of slide layouts, master slides, animations, and video integration.
Expertise in using design software such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for creating marketing materials.
Solid understanding of design principles, typography, color theory, and visual hierarchy.
Strong aesthetic sense and attention to detail, with the ability to translate complex ideas into visually engaging presentations.
Proficiency in creating proposals and other marketing materials in MS Word.
Experience in designing reusable templates, layouts, and master decks for efficient presentation design.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in a team environment.
Responsibilities

Create visually appealing and engaging PowerPoint presentations that effectively communicate key messages, utilizing a variety of visual elements such as icons, images, charts, and graphs.
Collaborate with stakeholders to understand their requirements, objectives, and target audience, translating them into visually impactful presentations that align with the company’s branding guidelines.
Demonstrate a strong sense of design and layout, ensuring consistency, balance, and visual hierarchy in all presentation materials.
Apply design principles to enhance the aesthetic appeal and readability of presentations, utilizing appropriate typography, color schemes, and imagery.
Incorporate animation effects and videos into presentations to enhance engagement and convey complex concepts effectively.
Develop and design marketing collateral, including mailers, posters, infographics, and social media graphics, using software such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
Design proposals and other marketing materials in MS Word, ensuring a professional and visually appealing layout that aligns with the company’s branding guidelines.
Create reusable templates, layouts, and master decks in PowerPoint for efficient and consistent presentation design across the organization.
Stay updated with the latest design trends, techniques, and tools, and incorporate them into presentations to maintain a fresh and modern look.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including copywriters, marketers, and project managers, to ensure that design concepts and deliverables meet project objectives and deadlines.
Conduct regular quality checks to ensure accuracy, consistency, and adherence to brand guidelines in all design deliverables.
