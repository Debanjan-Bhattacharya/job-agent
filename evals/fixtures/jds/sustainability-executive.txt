Job description
Sustainability Executive
Job Requirements
1.      Leading and being accountable for Sustainability Reporting and assessments including Materiality Assessment
2.      Ensure facilitation of high quality Sustainability reporting by facilitating company wide program.
3.      Enable participating in various external disclosures and assessments
4.      Co-ordinating with stakeholders for accurate data collection/management and subsequent reporting
5.      Internal Communication on sustainability projects; implemented and ongoing
6.      Facilitating in taking forward third party ratings such as MSCI, S&P, HRW, etc.
7.      Build Data systems for data collation and integration
8.      Audits across Energy, Water and Plastics
9. Work on Scope 3 data
10.      Support rollout and implementation of the Supply chain sustainability programs under Pragati 4P framework across divisions and the supplier value chain.
Work Experience
Education:
Graduate / Post Graduate, preferably in sustainability/Environment related
Any certification/course in environment science/sustainability would be an added advantage
Experience:
5-10 years of varied experience or 2-3 experience in BRSR/GRI disclosure specific areas and participation in BRSR assurance
Strong Experience in project/program management and research/consulting involving teams and multiple stakeholders
Knowledge:
Theoretical and applied knowledge in sustainability (Carbon footprint, water neutral, recycling, circular economy, waste management etc),
Understanding of various disclosures and subjects including IR, BRSR, GRI, SDGs, Human Rights etc
Behavioural Skills:

Passionate about sustainability and green initiatives
Influencing skills
Self-driven and action oriented
Data orientation and analysis
Ability to work in collaboration and with cross functional teams
High sense of accountability
Delivery / outcome focused
