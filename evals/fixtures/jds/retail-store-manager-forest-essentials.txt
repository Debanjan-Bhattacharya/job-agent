Job description
About the Role
At Forest Essentials, we believe luxury lies in authenticity, craftsmanship, and exceptional customer experience.
As an Assistant Store Manager, you will play a key role in supporting store operations, driving sales, and ensuring that every customer experiences the essence of our brand. You will work closely with the Store Manager to lead the team and uphold the highest standards of luxury retail.
Key Responsibilities
Store Operations

Support the Store Manager in daily store operations
Ensure smooth opening & closing procedures
Maintain SOP compliance and operational excellence
Sales & Performance

Drive individual and store sales targets
Maximize conversions through effective customer engagement
Upsell and cross-sell products aligned with customer needs
Team Leadership

Assist in managing and motivating the store team
Support in staff training, grooming, and performance monitoring
Ensure proper rostering and staff availability
Customer Experience

Deliver a personalized and luxurious in-store experience
Handle customer queries and resolve concerns effectively
Build long-term customer relationships and loyalty
Visual Merchandising & Brand Standards

Ensure VM guidelines are implemented as per brand standards
Maintain impeccable store aesthetics and hygiene
Uphold grooming and presentation standards of the team
Inventory & Compliance

Assist in stock management and replenishment
Support in audits and inventory checks
Minimize stock discrepancies and shrinkage
Candidate Profile

36 years of experience in retail
Minimum 1 year experience as Assistant Store Manager / Senior Sales Associate
Background in luxury / premium retail (beauty, skincare, fashion, lifestyle) preferred
Strong team handling and customer engagement skills
Passion for beauty, wellness, and customer service
Qualification

Graduate in any discipline

Strong leadership and ownership mindset
