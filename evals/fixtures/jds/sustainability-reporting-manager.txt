Sustainability Reporting Manager
Location: Mumbai, India | Full-time | Hybrid

About the Role:
We are seeking an experienced Sustainability Reporting Manager to own our ESG disclosure program in alignment with BRSR (Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting) mandates and GRI Standards.

Key Responsibilities:
- Prepare and publish annual BRSR and GRI-aligned sustainability reports
- Coordinate with finance, legal, HR, and operations to collect accurate ESG data
- Monitor developments in SEBI BRSR regulations, GRI updates, TCFD recommendations, and CDP frameworks
- Manage engagement with external assurance providers (Big 4 or specialist ESG assurance firms)
- Interface with rating agencies including MSCI ESG, Sustainalytics, and EcoVadis
- Lead carbon accounting across Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions
- Present ESG performance to Board ESG Committee and senior leadership quarterly

Requirements:
- GRI-certified professional or equivalent credential (BRSR, TCFD, CDP framework expertise essential)
- 7–12 years in corporate sustainability, ESG consulting, or investor relations with ESG focus
- Deep familiarity with SEBI BRSR mandate and Indian regulatory landscape
- Experience managing sustainability ratings and responding to MSCI or Sustainalytics questionnaires
- Strong understanding of carbon accounting methodologies (GHG Protocol)
- MBA, MSc in Sustainability/Environment, or equivalent qualification

Preferred:
- Experience with Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) commitments
- Prior exposure to integrated reporting (IR framework)
